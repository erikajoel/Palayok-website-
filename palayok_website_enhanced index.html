Welcome to Palayok

Experience the rich flavors of the Philippines at Palayok. We serve home-cooked Filipino dishes made with love and tradition.

Our History

Palayok was founded with a mission to bring the warmth of Filipino home cooking to every table. Our recipes are passed down through generations, ensuring authenticity and quality.

Location

Find us in General Santos City, the heart of Filipino cuisine.

Our Menu

Enjoy our best-selling Filipino dishes, each prepared with traditional ingredients and passion.

Adobo

Slow-cooked pork or chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, and spices.

Sinigang

A tangy tamarind-based soup with vegetables and your choice of meat.

Kare-Kare

A rich peanut stew with oxtail, tripe, and vegetables.

Lechon

Crispy and juicy roasted pig, served with a tangy vinegar dipping sauce.

Halo-Halo

A Filipino shaved ice dessert with mixed fruits, beans, and a scoop of ice cream.

Refreshing Drinks

Pair your meal with our delicious beverages, each offering a taste of Filipino culture.

Buko Juice

Fresh coconut juice, served straight from the coconut.

Calamansi Juice

A tangy, citrusy drink made from the Philippine lime, perfect for a refreshing kick.

Sago't Gulaman

A sweet, refreshing drink with tapioca pearls and jelly cubes.

Barako Coffee

A strong, bold coffee from the Batangas region, perfect for an energy boost.

Tsokolate Tablea

Traditional Filipino hot chocolate made from pure cacao, a rich, velvety treat.
